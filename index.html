Covid-19 Pandemic

A Global Pandemic That Stalled 2020.

As the deadly novel Covid-19 virus spreads it’s effect over the world and it’s people, there should be a constant need for recovery and alternative solutions in it’s wake. As social distancing and ‘work from home’ are common measures today, we are striving to develop strategies for when the world is ready for human contact again in the near future. We are preparing to face the world with a completely new approach, and as offices, workplaces, institutions, businesses & travel prepare to reopen, the need for newer, better sanitation procedures has never been greater. The question at hand is of human safety and health which should be the top most priority.

Acti – Clean is one such body that is working on research towards the development of safety and sanitaon measures and equipment for the common public in such times.

UVC Disinfectant System

Since its invention in early 1900, UVC lamps have become a staple method ofsterilization – one used in hospitals, airplanes, offices, and factories every day. UVC rays whose wavelength of 222 nanometers makes them safe for humans but still lethal to viruses. UVC Ray Kills 99.99% of Germs, Bacteria & Viruses. These lamps were used to cure another Coronavirus called SARS. The radiation warps the structure of their genetic material and prevents the viral particles from making more copies of themselves. That means they could be used in closed and crowded spaces where contamination risks run high, with potentially huge promise for use during the current pandemic. Act-Clean is the first company to make a specialized UV chamber which can be opted as a Stand Alone Chamber or can be combined with the Disinfection Spray Tunnel Providing Double Protection.
